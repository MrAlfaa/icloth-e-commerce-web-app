The future of fabric protection

Why Hydrophobic ?


The whole production process is naturally pollution-free and the clothing does no harm to the human body. These fabrics are finished with eco-friendly, high-performing repellent technology that makes water bead up and roll off the fabric. Each product in this collection is uniquely engineered from fine polyester fibers that increase breathability and moisture transmission. They are soft, extremely durable and easy to sew.


Polyester

polyester fabrics don’t absorb perspiration, or other fluids, leaving the wearer with a moist, clammy feel. Polyester fibers typically have a low level of wicking. Relative to cotton, polyester is stronger, with a greater ability to stretch. Fiber strength can range from 2.5 grams/denier to 9.5 grams/denier.


Acrylic

Acrylic fabric is made out of acrylic yarns woven or knitted into thick, warm material. This material provides incredible warmth and insulation, making it a popular choice for sweaters, coat linings, and outdoor athletic apparel. This fabric often serves as a cheaper alternative to wool


Modacrylic

Bright color, draping and dynamic, suitable for underwear, sweaters and clothing and also soft, waxy sense, suitable for men’s underwear applicable for knitwear, sportswear, underwear, sweater and other clothing products.
